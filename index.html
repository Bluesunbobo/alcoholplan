No Data Collection / 不收集个人数据
微醺志是一款纯本地工具。 我们不会收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。我们不使用任何第三方分析或追踪服务（如 Google Analytics 或 Firebase）。您输入的数据（如饮酒记录、体重、性别等）仅在您的本地设备上进行处理和存储。
Druk is a local-only utility tool. We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We DO NOT use any third-party analytics or tracking services. All data you input (e.g., drinks, weight, gender) is processed and stored ONLY on your local device.
Local Storage / 本地数据存储
App 内生成的所有记录和数据均使用 Apple 的 Core Data 技术存储在您的 iPhone 上。这些数据永远不会离开您的设备，开发者或任何第三方均无法访问。
All records and data generated within the app are stored using Apple's Core Data technology on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer or any third party.

The app does not request access to your Location, Contacts, Camera, or Microphone. We only request notification permission if necessary to provide metabolic alerts on your device.
