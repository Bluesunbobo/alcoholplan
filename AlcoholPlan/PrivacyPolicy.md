# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新: April 22, 2026**

Welcome to **Druk (微醺志)**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

欢迎使用 **Druk (微醺志)**。我们非常尊重并致力于通过遵守本政策来保护您的隐私。

## 1. Local Storage & Photos / 存储与相册权限

**Druk is a local-only utility tool.**
*   **Data Storage:** All records and data generated within the app are stored using Apple's Core Data technology on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device.
*   **Photo Library:** We request "Add to Photo Library" permission **ONLY** to save your generated posters. We **DO NOT** read, access, or upload your existing photos or videos.

**微醺志是一款纯本地工具。**
*   **数据存储：** App 内生成的所有记录和数据均使用 Apple 的 Core Data 技术存储在您的 iPhone 上。这些数据永远不会离开您的设备。
*   **相册权限：** 我们请求“写入相册”权限 **仅** 用于保存生成的分享海报。我们 **不会** 读取、访问或上传您现有的照片或视频。

## 2. No Data Collection / 不收集个人数据

We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We **DO NOT** use any third-party analytics or tracking services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase).

我们 **不会** 收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。我们 **不使用** 任何第三方分析或追踪服务（如 Google Analytics 或 Firebase）。

## 3. No Account System / 无账号体系说明

Druk does not have an account registration system. No user credentials or profiles are stored on any server. Consequently, account deletion is not applicable. All local data is permanently destroyed when the app is uninstalled.

本应用无账号体系，无需注册。不涉及账号注销或远程数据删除。卸载 App 即可彻底销毁本地存储的所有数据且不可恢复。

## 4. Regional Legal Standards / 地区法律标准区别

Druk includes a built-in database of legal DUI standards for 17+ countries. The app automatically applies jurisdiction-specific warnings and restrictions for alcohol-prohibited regions to ensure legal compliance.

本应用内置 17 个以上国家的法律标准数据库。App 会根据用户选择的地区自动应用特定的法律警示和功能限制（如针对禁酒地区的强制警示），以确保符合当地法律合规要求。

## 5. Content Sharing / 内容分享声明

Any "Poster Sharing" is handled entirely through the iOS system-level sharing components. Druk **DO NOT** host, upload, or distribute any user-generated content.

海报分享通过 iOS 系统原生组件完成。本 App **不托管**、不上传、不分发任何用户内容。

## 6. Age Requirement / 年龄要求

This app is strictly for adults who have reached the legal drinking age in their respective countries. We do not knowingly target or collect data from minors.

本应用仅限已达到所在国家法定饮酒年龄的成年人使用。我们不会故意针对未成年人。
