# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**生效日期 / Effective Date: 2026-04-18**

欢迎使用 Druk (醉好时光)。我们深知个人隐私对您的重要性，并致力于保护您的数据安全。

Welcome to Druk. We understand that your privacy is important to you and we are committed to protecting your data.

---

### 1. 数据收集与存储 / Data Collection and Storage

**核心原则：全本地运行**
Druk 是一款“离线优先”的应用。
- **个人生理数据**：您在初始设置中输入的体重、性别、代谢率等数据仅保存在您设备的本地存储（UserDefaults/AppStorage）中。
- **饮酒记录**：您的所有饮酒数据均保存在您设备本地的数据库（Core Data）中。

**Core Principle: Local Processing**
Druk is an "offline-first" application.
- **Biometric Data**: Weight, gender, and metabolic rate entered during setup are stored strictly on your device's local storage.
- **Drinking Logs**: All session records and logs are stored in your device's local database (Core Data).

---

### 2. 数据传输与分享 / Data Transmission and Sharing

**我们不会将您的数据上传到任何服务器。**
- 我们没有云端存储，也不会收集您的任何个人信息用于分析或广告。
- **用户自发分享**：当您点击“生成海报”或“系统分享”时，应用会生成图片。这些图片仅在您点击分享操作时，通过 iOS 系统原生接口传输给您选择的第三方应用（如微信、相册等）。Druk 本身不会保留或监控这些分享行为。

**We do NOT upload your data to any servers.**
- We have no cloud storage and do not collect any personal information for analytics or advertising.
- **User-Initiated Sharing**: When you generate posters or use system sharing, the app creates images. These are only transmitted to third-party apps (e.g., Photos, WeChat) via native iOS interfaces when you explicitly trigger the share action. Druk does not monitor or retain logs of these actions.

---

### 3. 数据安全与删除 / Data Security and Deletion

由于所有数据都在您的手机本地，数据安全由您设备的系统（iOS）保障。
- 如果您删除了 Druk 应用，iOS 系统会自动删除该应用关联的所有本地数据。
- 您也可以在应用内的“历史”界面手动删除特定记录，或在“设置”中点击“重启旅程”来清空所有数据。

Since all data is local, its security is maintained by your device's operating system (iOS).
- Deleting the Druk app will automatically remove all associated local data.
- You can manually delete records in the "History" screen or use "RESTART JOURNEY" in Settings to wipe all data.
