  *My heart’s guard has softened; the tenderness and longing I’ve hidden for a long time, I finally dare to let you catch steadily.*
- 酒懂我的欲言又止，而你，懂我没说出口的渴望与温柔。
  *Wine understands my unspoken words, and you understand the longing and tenderness I didn’t say.*
- 平时装了太久的懂事，此刻只想独占你所有的温柔，只想靠在你怀里。
  *I’ve pretended to be sensible for too long; right now I just want to monopolize all your tenderness, just want to lean in your arms.*
- 我给了所有人体面，唯独想把最不讲理的撒娇、最柔软的自己，都给你。
  *I gave decency to everyone, but only want to give you the most unreasonable coquetry, the softest self.*
- 懂事了一天，终于敢对你说，我也累了，想被你抱着，想被你疼。
  *After a day of being sensible, I finally dare to say to you, I’m also tired, want to be held by you, want to be loved by you.*

#### 0.050% 哲学黄金点
- 人类血液中天生缺少0.05%的酒精，今宵，我终于补全了这份柔软的本真。
  *Humans are born lacking 0.05% alcohol in their blood; tonight, I finally make up for this soft authenticity.*
- 0.05%的黄金时刻，是我终于不用再伪装懂事，不用再照顾所有人的情绪，只做我自己。
  *The golden moment of 0.05% is that I finally don’t have to pretend to be sensible, don’t have to take care of everyone’s emotions, just be myself.*
- 那个缺少的，从来都不是酒精，是那些我一直没机会感受的情绪，此刻终于敢流动了。
  *What was missing was never alcohol, but the feelings I never had a chance to feel; they finally dare to flow now.*
- 这是黄金点，我此刻感受到的所有温柔、所有心动、所有情绪，都是真实的，不是借口。
  *This is the golden point; all the tenderness, heartbeat, and emotions I feel right now are real, not excuses.*
- 微醺不是软弱，是我终于允许自己，把藏了太久的情绪，全部释放出来。
  *Tipsiness is not weakness; it’s that I finally allow myself to release all the emotions I’ve hidden for too long.*

#### 0.0%–0.07% 微醺高光区
- 喝的不是酒，是一肚子没说出口的委屈，是藏了太久的温柔。
  *What I drink is not wine, but a stomach full of unspoken grievances, and tenderness hidden for too long.*
- 懂事了太久，终于敢在微醺里，不乖一次，软一次。
  *I’ve been sensible for too long; finally dare to be not good, be soft once in tipsiness.*
- 【祝酒词】敬温柔，敬懂得，敬同频的共鸣，敬所有被看见的情绪。
  *[Toast] To tenderness, to understanding, to resonance at the same frequency, to all emotions that are seen.*
- 被人懂，比喝酒更让人上头；被人疼，比微醺更让人安心。
  *Being understood is more intoxicating than drinking; being loved is more reassuring than tipsiness.*
- 成年人的崩溃，都藏在酒后的沉默里；成年人的治愈，都藏在被懂得的温柔里。
  *The breakdown of adults is hidden in the silence after drinking; the healing of adults is hidden in the understood tenderness.*
- 酒懂我的欲言又止，懂我的言不由衷，懂我所有没说出口的话。
  *Wine understands my unspoken words, my insincere words, all the words I didn’t say.*
- 我给了所有人温柔，今晚，终于想把温柔，都留给自己。
  *I gave tenderness to everyone; tonight, I finally want to leave all the tenderness to myself.*

#### 0.07%–0.08% 🚨 预警
- 情绪可以释放，但别让酒精，放大你的委屈，伤了你的心。
  *Emotions can be released, but don’t let alcohol amplify your grievances and hurt your heart.*
- 你还清醒，但你的情绪已经在过载边缘，别让自己陷进去。
  *You’re still lucid, but your emotions are on the edge of overload; don’t let yourself fall into it.*
- 温柔要有底线，爱自己要有边界，微醺刚好，过则伤身。
  *Tenderness must have a bottom line, loving yourself must have boundaries; tipsiness is just right, excess hurts the body.*

#### 0.08%–0.10% ⚠️ 提醒
- 别在深夜做决定，别在喝醉时动真心，别用酒精惩罚自己。
  *Don’t make decisions late at night, don’t give your heart when drunk, don’t punish yourself with alcohol.*
- 你要的是被心疼，不是喝到醉，才换来的虚假关注。
  *What you want is to be loved dearly, not the false attention you get only when you’re drunk.*
- 喝到失态，你之前所有的温柔、所有的体面，都会被人忘记。
  *If you drink until you lose your manners, all the tenderness and decency you had before will be forgotten.*

#### ≥0.10% 🛑 劝诫
- 停下来，没有人值得你，用健康、用尊严、用身体去换一句心疼。
  *Stop. No one is worth you exchanging your health, dignity, and body for a word of distress.*
- 爱自己，是终身浪漫的开始，别用酒精，伤害那个已经很努力的自己。
  *Loving yourself is the beginning of lifelong romance; don’t use alcohol to hurt the self that has worked very hard.*
- 喝到麻木，就没人能看见你心里的伤了，也没人能接住你的温柔了。
  *If you drink until you’re numb, no one can see the hurt in your heart, and no one can catch your tenderness.*
