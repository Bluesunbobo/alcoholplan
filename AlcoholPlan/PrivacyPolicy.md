# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新: April 22, 2026**

Welcome to **Druk (微醺志)**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

欢迎使用 **Druk (微醺志)**。我们非常尊重并致力于通过遵守本政策来保护您的隐私。

## 1. Local Storage / 数据本地存储

**Druk is a local-only utility tool.** All records and data generated within the app are stored using Apple's Core Data technology on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer or any third party.

**微醺志是一款纯本地工具。** App 内生成的所有记录和数据均使用 Apple 的 Core Data 技术存储在您的 iPhone 上。这些数据永远不会离开您的设备，开发者或任何第三方均无法访问。

## 2. No Data Collection / 不收集个人数据

We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We **DO NOT** use any third-party analytics or tracking services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase).

我们**不会**收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。我们**不使用**任何第三方分析或追踪服务（如 Google Analytics 或 Firebase）。

## 3. No Account System / 无账号体系说明

Druk does not have an account registration system. No user credentials or profiles are stored on any server. Consequently, account deletion is not applicable. All local data is permanently destroyed when the app is uninstalled.

本应用无账号体系，无需注册。不涉及账号注销或远程数据删除。卸载 App 即可彻底销毁本地存储的所有数据且不可恢复。

## 4. Content Sharing / 内容分享声明

Any "Poster Sharing" or "Moment Sharing" is handled entirely through the iOS system-level sharing components. Druk **DO NOT** host, upload, or distribute any user-generated content. As we do not host content, user-reporting or blocking mechanisms are not applicable.

海报分享通过 iOS 系统原生组件完成。本 App 不托管、不上传、不分发任何用户生成的内容（UGC）。由于我们不托管内容，因此不设 UGC 举报或屏蔽机制。

## 5. Legal Compliance / 法律合规

Druk provides alcohol tracking and legal DUI standard references for educational purposes. It includes mandatory alerts for regions where alcohol is prohibited. By using this app, you agree to comply with all local laws and regulations.

微醺志提供酒精追踪和法律酒驾标准参考，仅用于科普教育目的。它包含针对禁酒地区的强制性警告。使用本应用即表示您同意遵守所有当地法律法规。

## 6. Age Requirement / 年龄要求

This app is strictly for adults who have reached the legal drinking age in their respective countries. We do not knowingly target or collect data from minors.

本应用仅限已达到所在国家法定饮酒年龄的成年人使用。我们不会故意针对未成年人或收集其数据。
